Use cases

Who measures by air

Anywhere material moves and money follows it, measured volumes settle the conversation.

  • Aggregate producers — inventory and month-end reconciliation
  • Earthworks contractors — cut/fill progress and pay quantities
  • Landfills and soil depots — airspace and capacity tracking
  • Winter operations — salt and sand stock verification

Method

Same flight, same basis, every time

Repeat surveys fly the identical flight plan with RTK positioning, so period-over-period comparisons measure your material — not survey noise. Base surfaces and toe definitions are documented in every report, making the numbers defensible in an audit or dispute.
