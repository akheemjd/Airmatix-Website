Where it fits

Inspection flights replace the slow, expensive part of looking at a structure: getting a person up there. Roof condition ahead of a re-roofing decision, façade reviews, tower and antenna checks, post-storm documentation — captured in a visit, without shutting anything down.

  • Roof condition assessments and re-roofing scoping
  • Building façade and envelope reviews
  • Tower, mast and antenna checks
  • Post-storm and insurance-claim documentation

A drone inspection is a visual record. Where a program requires hands-on testing or an engineer's stamp, our imagery supports that work rather than replacing it — and it usually shortens the time a person has to spend at height.
